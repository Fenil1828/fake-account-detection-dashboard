CSV Format Requirements

Your CSV should include these columns:

  • username - Account username
  • followers_count - Number of followers
  • following_count - Number following
  • posts_count - Total posts
  • account_age_days - Account age in days
  • has_profile_pic - true/false
  • bio - Profile bio text
  • avg_likes_per_post - Average likes
  • burst_posting_score - 0-1 (higher = more suspicious)
  • spam_word_count - Spam indicators
  • duplicate_content_ratio - 0-1
